1. Introduction

Definition of AI

Definition of AI

Definition: Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. The term may also be applied to any machine that exhibits traits associated with a human mind such as learning and problem-solving.
  • Automation: AI can make repeated things autonomous.

Science vs Technology

  • Science: Science is a systematic approach to understanding the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis.
  • Technology: Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes.

Science vs Technology

Science
Technology
Focus
Understanding nature

Technology vs Application

  • Technology: Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes.
  • Application: Application is the use of technology for a specific purpose.

History of AI

  • 1950s: Alan Turing (Turing Test): Machines could simulate human intelligence
  • 1956: Dartmouth Conference: AI was born
  • 1980s: Expert System
  • 1990s: Machine Learning
  • 21 Century: Deep Learning, Big Data, Neural Network, NLP
